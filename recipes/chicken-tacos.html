Chicken Tacos

Description

These easy slow cooker chicken tacos are easy to make with just 3 ingredients in your crockpot. Spoon the filling into warm tortillas for a very tasty meal any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • Broth
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Chicken
  • Taco Shells

Steps

  1. Season the chicken.
  2. Place in the slow cooker.
  3. Pour the broth over the chicken.
  4. Cook on Low for 6-8 hours.
  5. Shred the chicken.
  6. Spoon the chicken into the Taco Shells.
